Dylan Hutchison

SIGHTS & SMELLS

Design project tasked with creating a perfume, and designing corresponding container and packaging. This is VUUR, a woodland natural musk made from petrichor,  pine, and burning firewood.

CLASSICAL BOOK DESIGN

A redesign in the classical page layout of the epic poem The Wasteland, by T.S. Eliot. The major challenge of this project was to accurately convert a stanza format into legible long-form paragraphs while keeping the translation and pacing.

PARADEISOS WINERY

A fictional winery based in on the island of Crete, specializing in the rosé wine Retsina Anagénnisi. The label combines the anitque colors with a vivid and modern layout.
